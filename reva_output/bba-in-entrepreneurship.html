  • Programme Specific Outcomes

Course Duration

6 Semesters
(3 years)

Eligibility Criteria

B.B.A. Pass in PUC / 10+2 with an aggregate of 50% of any recognised Board / Council or any other qualification recognized as equivalent there to

Overview

REVA University offers a meticulously designed BBA in Entrepreneurship programme meant to produce creative leaders with forward-looking ideas. This undergraduate degree prepares students to negotiate the exciting world of startups and enterprise development by combining basic business knowledge with specific entrepreneurial strategy training. The curriculum covers a wide range of topics, including company planning, innovation management, venture finance, market analysis, and leadership development, while promoting a complete knowledge of the entrepreneurial ecosystem.

Experiential learning opportunities such as live projects, case studies, and industry expert interactions provide students with practical experience in ideating, developing, and running business enterprises successfully. The modern infrastructure and mentoring-driven strategy of REVA University help create an atmosphere suitable for developing the critical thinking, problem-solving, and decision-making abilities required for entrepreneurial success.

Graduates are ready to start their own companies, grow family enterprises, or perform brightly in jobs needing creative ideas within contemporary companies. At REVA University, the BBA in Entrepreneurship offers a transforming journey that enables students to significantly influence the global business scenario. Emphasizing resilience, innovation, and sustainability, this program provides the groundwork for would-be business owners to create an almost endless future.

Programme Educational Objectives (PEOs)

PEO-1

Graduates after successful completion of the programme will be able to take up career in Public, Private and Corporate organizations.

PEO-2

Graduates after completion of the programme will be able to work with diverse teams and are expected to take better decisions in complex situations.

PEO-3

Graduates will be able to incorporate social, ethical and moral principles in professional and personal life.

Programme Outcomes (POs)

The School of Management studies was established in the year 2014 and since then the School has defined Programme Outcomes for the courses offered during the Board of Studies meeting. Based on the advice and suggestions of the BOS, EIGHT Programme Outcomes have been formulated, keeping in view the Vision, Mission and Programme Educational Objectives.

PO 1

Communicate effectively with various stake - holders

PO 2

Foster analytical and critical thinking abilities for data – based decision making

PO 3

Ability to develop Value based leadership ability

PO 4

Ability to understand, analyze and communicate global, economic, legal and ethical aspects of business

PO 5

Engage in Research and apply statistical tools and techniques for problem solving and decision making.

PO 6

Enhance skills required for a particular domain by integrating practical and theoretical knowledge

PO 7

Gain knowledge through inter-disciplinary and multi–disciplinary courses

PO 8

Apply knowledge of Theory and Practices to solve business problems

Programme Specific Outcomes

  • PSO1: Demonstrate understanding of the basic concepts and theoretical knowledge used in the different commerce and business related areas.
  • PSO2: Develop ideas for start-ups through knowledge and skills developed during the course of the degree.
  • PSO3: Apply different tools and techniques in solving problems related to Business and in day to day situations during their career

Career Opportunities

A BBA in Entrepreneurship enables graduates to access a wide range of exciting future prospects, capacitating them to concentrate on their business acumen and creativity. Equipped with strategic knowledge and creative thinking, graduates after a BBA in Entrepreneurship may start their own businesses, stimulate invention, and produce significant ideas. On the other hand, if entrepreneurial abilities are widely appreciated, they might perform in professions such as venture capital analysts, business development managers, or innovation consultants.

The degree also offers a robust foundation for expanding family-owned companies or flourishing within companies that give strategic development a top priority. There are many opportunities at incubators, accelerators, and consulting companies that let graduates help others in their entrepreneurial endeavors. This flexible certification in REVA University not only promotes freedom but also helps people to be drivers of economic development and innovation.
