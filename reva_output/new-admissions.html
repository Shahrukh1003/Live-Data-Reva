REVA University

REVA University is a State Private University established in Karnataka State under the Government of Karnataka Act No. 13 in the year 2012 in Bengaluru. REVA University, recognised by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), has an A+ rating from NAAC. As per the QS Asian University Rankings 2023, REVA is ranked 6th among State Private Universities in Karnataka and 47th in India. The University is spread over 45 acres of land and has a sprawling green campus. With an array of programmes ranging from Engineering to Architecture to Technology to Management to Humanities, Legal Studies, Applied Sciences, Allied Health Science, Sports Science to Design and Performing Arts and Indic Studies, we are a composite campus located in the heart of the city.
